Considerations

What i love about doing my job is to always find a way to not only resolve the client’s problems, but bring the best code with good & clean practices that makes every line easily maintainable. I really like well documented code.

Software development, for me, means that we are trying to make robots make robot’s things, such as: automatization, huge information storage & repetitive processes. People are keys in this area, because we are solving problems from our world, right? Getting the best from each human to figure out how to solve these problems makes a good software.

I really like companies that invest in bringing the best to their teams: courses, talks and even food! For the simple reason that if we give due value to every person who helps in the process, we will do everything we need in the best possible way.
